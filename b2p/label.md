# Binary-2-Path (b2p)

> The master chef for your binaries. Fresh installs, zero hassle.

## ✨ Highlights

* **Portable Environment:** Install apps anywhere without polluting your global PATH manually.
* **Recipe Driven:** Every app is a carefully crafted recipe, ensuring consistent results.
* **Lightweight:** No heavy dependencies, just pure binary management.

## 🚀 Quick Taste

Order your first package with these commands:

    b2p search favorite-app
    b2p install favorite-app

## 📝 About

b2p was born to simplify how developers and power users manage their tools. Instead of messy installers, we provide a clean, "gastronomic" approach to software: you choose the dish, we follow the recipe and serve it hot in your terminal.
